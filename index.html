ABOUT

My name is Soliu Adeosun, a Front-end developer with expertise in JavaScript, HTML, Python, Vanilla CSS, Tailwind CSS, Git, and Github.
I am currently interning at TIIDELab, where I'm putting my skills to use and expanding my knowledge.

I have a track record of successful contributions to projects like a Qr code generator and virtual reality website. Apart from these projects, I've engaged in honing my skills through personal projects, team projects, and open-source projects.

If you're in need of a Front-end developer to join your team or assist with a project, I'm just a message away.
